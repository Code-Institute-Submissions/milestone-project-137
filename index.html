How to play:

The aim of the game is to find all matching lego character pairs before the time runs out. Click on a card to turn it over, then click on another one. If the two cards match, they will stay face-up. If the two cards do not match, they will return to being face-down.

The game ends when all the cards have been matched in pairs before time finished

Scoring system:

Game will start with 0 points, score 50 points whenever you turn over cards that match and lose 20 points whenever they don't match.

Time remaining when all pairs matched will be added as bonus:

  • Easy: 1 sec = 1 point
  • Medium: 1 sec = 2 points
  • Hard: 1 sec = 3 points
